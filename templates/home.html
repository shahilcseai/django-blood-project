Why Donate Blood?

Your donation is essential to maintain the blood supply needed to save lives.

Save Lives

A single donation can save up to three lives, helping accident victims, surgical patients, and those battling cancer.

Quick Process

The donation process takes only about 10-15 minutes. The entire visit, including registration and refreshments, takes about an hour.

Regular Need

Blood has a limited shelf life and must be constantly replenished to ensure hospitals have adequate supplies for all patients.

Donation Process

Learn what to expect when you donate blood

Step 1
Registration

You'll sign in, show ID, and read some information about donating blood.

Step 2
Health History

We'll ask questions about your health history and check your temperature, pulse, blood pressure, and hemoglobin levels.

Step 3
Donation

The actual donation takes about 10-15 minutes. You'll be seated comfortably while a pint of blood is drawn.

Step 4
Refreshments

After donating, you'll have a snack and drink to help replenish fluids before leaving.
